Definition 3.5. Adding Vectors and Multiplying a Vector by a Number.

These two operations have the obvious definitions
\begin{align*} \va=\llt a_1,a_2\rgt,\ \vb =\llt b_1,b_2\rgt \amp\amp \implies\amp\amp \va+\vb=\llt a_1+b_1,a_2+b_2\rgt\\ \va=\llt a_1,a_2\rgt,\ s\text{ a number} \amp\amp \implies\amp\amp s\va=\llt sa_1,sa_2\rgt \end{align*}
and similarly in three dimensions.
