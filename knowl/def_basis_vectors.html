Definition 3.9.

The standard basis vectors in two dimensions are
\begin{equation*} \hi = \llt 1,0\rgt \qquad\qquad\hj =\llt 0,1\rgt \end{equation*}
The standard basis vectors in three dimensions are
\begin{equation*} \hi = \llt 1,0,0\rgt \qquad\qquad\hj =\llt 0,1,0\rgt \qquad\qquad\hk =\llt 0,0,1\rgt \end{equation*}
