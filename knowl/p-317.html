We can create an intermediate transformation adding complimentary percentages \(p\) of the identity transformation and our matrix transformation, \((1-p)*I_n +p*A \text{.}\) We obtain a sliding scale between the two by incrementing through our percentage \(p\) from 0 to 1, starting with 100% of our identity transformation and ending with 100% of our matrix transformation.
