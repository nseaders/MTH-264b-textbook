Because of Property 7 of Theorem Theorem 3.14, the dot product can be used to test whether or not two vectors are perpendicular to each other. That is, whether or not the angle between the two vectors is \(90^\circ\text{.}\) Another name 8  for “perpendicular” is “orthogonal”. Testing for orthogonality is one of the main uses of the dot product.
